### USR
***
<code><var>value</var> <b>= USR</b>[<var>n</var>]<b>(</b><var>expr</var><b>)</b></code>

Calls a machine-code function and returns its return value.

#### Parameters
* <code><var>n</var></code> is a digit `<b>0</b> to <b>9</b>`.
* <code><var>expr</var></code> is an expression.

#### Errors
<code><var>n</var></code> is not a digit `[0 to 9]`: <samp>Syntax error</samp>.
